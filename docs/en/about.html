Our Philosophy

The End of Career Esotericism.

The C.O.R.E. Index was not developed to flatter you. We built it to make the invisible systemic and internal barriers visible that cause high performers to burn out every day.

Why we analyze instead of motivate.

The market for professional aptitude tests suffers from a fundamental design flaw: it is based on the principle of pleasing people.

Whether it is the simple division into four colorful types or the assignment of a flattering archetype from early 20th-century theories - most tools hand out compliments instead of revealing real causal relationships. They are often optimized to create a good mood in team-building workshops, not to explain deep professional dead ends.

The result is a culture of "toxic positivity." Exhausted professionals are told to simply "change their mindset" or "find their true passion," while they fail in dysfunctional corporate structures, under unclear management, or because of their own unconscious perfectionism. Real change, however, never begins with a compliment, but with an honest assessment.

Our Approach

We operate at the intersection of data architecture and workplace reality. Instead of inventing new abstract theories, we use the Big Five model (O.C.E.A.N.) as an uncompromising basis for everyday professional life.

The C.O.R.E. (Career Obstacle & Resilience Evaluation) Index is a radically pragmatic profiling tool. Our primary focus is not only on confirming your strengths, but above all on isolating your professional "shadow." We make measurable the extent to which unconscious factors such as fear of failure, concern about status loss, or poor boundary-setting (the classic helper syndrome) sabotage your economic and strategic decisions.

01. Empirical Basis

We avoid gut feeling and esoteric classifications. The C.O.R.E. analysis is based on the evaluation of standardized behavioral patterns. The underlying scales reflect an established psychological model that is considered stable and reliable worldwide.

02. Focus on the Obstacle

Anyone who wants to unlock their professional potential must first understand what is holding them back. We look at the dysfunctional patterns that arise especially in demanding work environments - from getting stuck in the "golden cage" to simply getting things done without real recognition.

03. Clear Consequence

At the end of the test, there are no abstract metaphors or animal comparisons. You receive executive action steps that objectively show you whether it is time to set boundaries, renegotiate budgets, or pull the ripcord.

Clarity and Boundaries.

True professionalism also shows in naming one's own limits precisely. C.O.R.E. is an operational analysis tool for the professional context, not a clinic and not a substitute for therapy.

What the analysis does

We isolate the connections between your working style and the systemic hurdles in your job. We provide a structured data basis for self-reflection, upcoming salary negotiations, or orientation when considering a professional realignment. It is about career strategy and self-management.

What the system explicitly does NOT do

We do not provide any medical, psychiatric, or clinical-psychological diagnoses. Our system does not treat conditions such as clinical depression, burnout syndrome, or anxiety disorders. In cases of health crises or psychological distress, we strongly advise consulting trained physicians and licensed psychotherapeutic professionals.
